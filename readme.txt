The character is controlled with standard FPS controls for movement, hold SHIFT to sprint.
To access the player's photocamera press the C key, an overlay will appear to let you know you are using the camera.
To zoom in and out while using the camera press the V and B keys.
To take a photo press the N key.
To spawn a bird press the I key.
When standing next to the computer press the E key to access it. 
Then to close it you'll need to hit the red X button with your cursor.
